Welcome to Tasty European Food

Tasty European Food is here from 2005 Russian & East European food shop which brings happines to everyone who used to buy similar food back home. It opened in 2005 in Finchley Central as a place where Russian, Romanian and Lithuanian speaking residents of England could buy and communicate with others in their native language. We are delighted to see you amongst our customers.
